European Facial

DEEP CLEANSING • ANTI AGEING

1hr 10mins+

A classic spa treatment focused on deep cleansing, exfoliation, and hydration of the face and neck. It typically includes cleansing, steaming, exfoliation, extractions, a mask, and massage. This comprehensive approach aims to rejuvenate the skin, improve its texture and tone, and promote a healthy, glowing complexion.

The treatment experience

A Deep-Down Cleansing

When you sit down for your European facial, you will most likely be in a reclined position, and your hair will be secured with a cap to prevent any chemicals from damaging the hair. Once you are made comfortable, the skin care specialist will cleanse your skin with an exfoliating scrub to slough off dead skin cells and expose healthy skin beneath. This part of the European facial treatment will also remove excess oil, dead skin, and even old makeup that may be clogging your pores.


The exfoliating step of the European facial technique will also encourage the skin to renew itself by creating new skin cells to repair the micro damage caused by the scrub. Once the exfoliating scrub is complete, the skin care specialist will steam your pores open to ensure that all the dirt and other debris has been swept away and that skin treatment ingredients can be absorbed to the fullest. Finally, this step of a European facial is completed by placing warm towels over the face to enhance the steaming process and the expansion of pores for 10-15 Min.


Extractions

If you suffer from any type of acne, the next step the skin care specialist will take in your European facial will be to remove all blackheads and whiteheads from your skin by way of extractions of the problem areas.


Massage and Face Mask

At this point during your European facial, your esthetician will apply a facial mask that is customized to match you specific skin type. Once the mask is applied evenly, it will be left in place for about fifteen minutes, during which time the European facial professional will give you a brief massage that will focus on the hands, neck. This step is meant to relax your mind and bring the therapeutic effects of the facial treatment together with the calming effects of the massage for a more unified rejuvenation experience. Once the mask is removed, the skin care specialist will conclude the European facial with a moisturizer and toner to prevent the skin from drying out, as a deep cleansing may often cause dryness. Pre and post care

What can I expect after my treatment?

+
After a European facial, you can generally expect an immediate improvement in skin hydration and a radiant, healthy glow. You might also experience a slight redness or sensitivity, especially if extractions were performed, but this should subside quickly. The skin will feel softer and smoother, and pores may appear minimized due to deep cleansing and exfoliation.

What precautions should I take pre and post?

+
Before a European facial, avoid harsh skincare products, especially exfoliants, and refrain from waxing or shaving facial hair for a few days to prevent irritation. After the facial, protect your skin from sun exposure, avoid touching your face, and don't apply makeup or other harsh products for a specified period, typically 6-12 hours or longer. Staying hydrated and moisturizing are also key to maintaining the benefits.

Common questions

Will my skin be red after a facial?

+

Slight redness is normal, especially after extractions, but should subside quickly.


Individuals with sensitive skin may experience more redness.

What are the benefits?

+

Unclog and shrink the size of pores: Facials are designed to clean your pores on a much deeper level than a typical daily rinse. Overtime, pores fill and widen with the buildup of dirt, oil and debris. Cleaning your pores helps to clear your skin and prevent acne on the face, which can thus change the size of your pores. Please keep in mind to not pick your skin, as it needs to be cleaned and handled by a professional.


Slow the aging process: Since facials keep your skin feeling healthier, rejuvenated, glowing, and smoother, you will feel younger as a result. Not only will you feel younger, but you will also look like it, as facials reduce age spots, hydrate the skin, reduce wrinkles, and promote collagen production. Collagen production is essential to the skin as it prevents premature aging, wrinkles, and sagging. By taking part in the facial process, facials help to reverse aging as nutrients and antioxidants enter the skin.


Even the skin tone: An increase in skin cell turnover is also responsible for evening the skin tone. As new skin cells reach the surface of your face, it makes your skin smoother and removes scars, blemishes, dark spots and more, which makes your skin softer and more even. Please note that dark spots largely come from the Sun’s UV rays, so even if you receive a facial, you should always make sure to wear sunscreen when exposed to the Sun; people of any age can receive dark spots.


Relieve face tension: Facials are good for your skin but they can also alleviate any pain in your muscles. The physical act of receiving a facial massage can actually help to reduce tension in your face and neck muscles. If you have been feeling stressed, a facial will ease your muscles and make you feel at peace.


What are the risks?

+

European facial treatments are generally safe, but some risks can occur, including allergic reactions, skin irritation, breakouts, and infections, especially with pre-existing skin conditions. Proper preparation and aftercare are crucial to minimize these risks. Avoid exfoliating products and waxing before the facial, and protect your skin from the sun afterward.

How many treatments will I need?

+

Monthly treatments are generally recommended to aid in skin cell turnover. This helps maintain a healthy glow and address issues like dullness and congestion.
