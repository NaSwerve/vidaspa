The treatment experience

Involves using a peel containing natural ingredients like algae and herbs to exfoliate the skin and stimulate collagen production. The process includes applying a liquid peel to the face, massaging it in, and then washing it off after a specific duration, often 5-15 minutes. The skin may feel hot, sensitive, or tingly during the treatment, which is a normal reaction. Post-treatment, the skin is expected to peel and flake, revealing newer, healthier skin underneath.

Benefits:

  • Exfoliation and Skin Renewal - Algae peels gently exfoliate the skin, removing dead skin cells and promoting the growth of new, healthy skin cells.
  • Reduced Hyperpigmentation - Algae extracts can help fade dark spots and even out skin tone by inhibiting the production of melanin.
  • Acne Treatment - The peels can help reduce sebum production, fight bacteria, and unclog pores, making them beneficial for acne-prone skin.
  • Anti-Aging Effects - Algae peels are rich in antioxidants that can combat free radicals, which contribute to premature aging. They can also stimulate collagen production, improving skin elasticity and firmness.
  • Hydration and Nourishment - Algae-based ingredients can help moisturize the skin and provide essential nutrients for a healthy complexion.

Pre and post care

What can I expect after my treatment?

+
After your treatment, you can expect some redness and sensitivity immediately following the procedure, which should subside within 12-24 hours. You will likely experience dryness and flaking of the skin as it regenerates, typically starting around day 3 and lasting for 3-7 days. It's crucial to avoid picking or pulling at the peeling skin, and to moisturize regularly to support the healing process.

What precautions should I take pre and post?

+

Before an Organic Algae Peel, avoid sun exposure, strenuous exercise, and harsh skincare products for a few days. It's also recommended to cease using retinol, acids, and other exfoliants prior to the treatment. Ensure your skin is well-hydrated and avoid alcohol consumption for 24 hours beforehand.


After an Organic Algae Peel, prioritize sun protection and avoid picking at your skin. It's crucial to hydrate, moisturize, and use gentle cleansers. Avoid direct sunlight, heat, and strenuous workouts for a few days. Don't exfoliate, and let any peeling skin shed naturally.
