The treatment experience

Involves using a peel containing natural ingredients, such as algae and herbs, to exfoliate the skin and stimulate collagen production. Pre and post care

Zena Algae Peeling After Care

+
  • Wash your face in the morning and at night with a gentle soap, pat it dry, and do not rub the skin.
  • Use only powder SPF 50 and above, and reapply every 2 hours.
  • Don’t use any form of moisturizer for 7 days
  • Don’t use any form of exfoliant for one month
  • Drink a lot of water
  • Don’t drink any alcoholic beverages for a week
  • Don’t get exposed to the sun
  • Don’t get exposed to any form of heat (cooking, sauna, hot shower, hair dryer)
  • Don’t do any form of exercise for one week
  • Don’t remove the peeling skin (old skin). The earlier removal can cause scars
  • Don’t do any procedure for 30 days
  • Don’t make use of any depilatory cream
  • Don’t dye the hair

Sensation after peeling

+

Felling of punctures, redness, tingling, heat, dryness, desquamation between 3 to 7 days.

Treatment contraindication

+

Vitiligo, rosacea, use of Roaccutane, herpes, cancer, telangiectasia, open wounds.
