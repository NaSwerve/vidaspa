The treatment experience

The esthetician applies a numbing cream to the face to help minimize any discomfort, and after about 30 minutes, the treatment begins. A small handheld device that has tiny needles on the end is used. The device moves across my face in a circular motion, creating small punctures on the surface of my skin.

Benefits:

  • Helps With Acne: If you are suffering from acne and acne scars, you know first hand how difficult it is to find an effective treatment. Microneedling treatments help reduce scars by improving the overall texture of your skin and helping your acne medication be more effective.
  • Reduces Blackheads: Tired of those little bumps on your nose? Microneedling opens pores, releasing clogged blackheads. After a month, you’ll notice a reduction in blackheads.
  • Assists Other Skincare Products: When you do microneedling, it creates micro-holes. When you apply any topical treatments after the microneedling session, your skin will receive them more easily and deliver them deeper into your dermis compared to regular application.
  • Reduces Wrinkles and Fine Lines: Lines and wrinkles are an inevitable part of aging. You have the freedom to leave them be or reduce their appearance. The tiny injuries from microneedling help boost elastin and collagen production and this helps fight wrinkles and fine lines.
  • Improves Skin Texture: As mentioned earlier, the skin’s regeneration after microneedling is the reason for all of its wonderful benefits. You’re likely to see improvements in the texture and tone of your skin after only a couple of sessions.

Pre and post care

What can I expect after my treatment?

+
After your treatment, you can expect some redness, swelling, and a sensation similar to a mild sunburn. These side effects are normal and typically subside within a few hours to a few days. You may also experience some tightness, dryness, or flaking as your skin heals. Avoid touching your face, using makeup, or engaging in strenuous exercise for at least 24-72 hours after the procedure to minimize irritation and infection risk.

What precautions should I take pre and post?

+

Pre-microneedling, avoid blood-thinning medications, excessive sun exposure, and harsh skincare products.


Post-microneedling, protect your skin from the sun, avoid strenuous exercise and sweating, and refrain from using active skincare ingredients. Gentle cleansing and hydration are key for optimal healing.

Common questions

Does microneedling hurt?

+

Microneedling may cause some discomfort, but it is generally tolerable. Numbing creams are often applied before the procedure to minimize any pain.

What are the benefits?

+

Microneedling can improve skin tone and texture, reduce the appearance of scars (including acne scars and stretch marks), minimize wrinkles and fine lines, and address hyperpigmentation.

What areas can be treated?

+

Microneedling can be performed on various areas of the body, including the face, neck, chest, and other areas with scars or stretch marks.

How many treatments will I need?

+

people typically get three to six sessions with a four- to six-week break in between each session. Sessions usually take about an hour from start to finish. The schedule and plan an aesthetician puts together is based off skin type and the results the patient is hoping to achieve.
