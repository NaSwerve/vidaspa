The treatment experience

Treatment required the use of numbing cream for 40 minutes before treatment. The device, equipped with tiny needles, is then used to create controlled micro-injuries in the skin. Simultaneously, RF energy is delivered deep into the skin, stimulating collagen production and tightening underlying tissues. During the treatment, a customized serum is selected specifically for your skin type and goals, and is applied. The micro-channels allow the serum to deeply penetrate the skin, enhancing its effectiveness and delivering powerful, targeted results . Exosome or PDNR can be added to this treatment. Consult with our skin specialist.

What to Know About Morpheus8

One of the key advantages to the Morpheus skin treatment is the deep, controlled delivery of radiofrequency energy, reducing the risk of post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation in all tones of skin. Because of the depth reached and the ability to customize the output of RF energy, Morpheus8 can be safely used on darker skin tones. Patients particularly prone to hyperpigmentation may be prescribed a hydroquinone cream or other pigment inhibitor before and after a Morpheus skin treatment.

Benefits:

  • Reduces wrinkles and fine lines - Morpheus8 can smooth out wrinkles and fine lines on the face and body, particularly around the eyes, mouth, and forehead.
  • Improves skin texture and tone - By stimulating collagen and elastin production, Morpheus8 can help to even out skin texture, reduce the appearance of pores, and improve overall skin tone.
  • Tightens loose or sagging skin - Morpheus8 can effectively tighten and lift loose skin on the face, neck, and body, providing a more youthful and contoured appearance.
  • Safe for all skin types - Morpheus8 is safe and effective for all skin types, including darker skin tones, due to its unique color-blind technology that minimizes the risk of hyperpigmentation.

Pre and post care

What can I expect after my treatment?

+
After your treatment, you can expect some redness, swelling, and a warm sensation in the treated area, similar to a sunburn, which should subside within a few hours to a few days. Some peeling or flaking may occur as your skin heals, and small scabs may form in the treated areas. Full results, including smoother, tighter skin, will develop gradually over the following weeks and months as collagen production increases.

What precautions should I take pre and post?

+

Pre-treatment precautions for Morpheus8 include avoiding sun exposure, tanning beds, and certain medications like blood thinners and retinoids for a specified period before the procedure


Post-treatment, it's crucial to protect your skin from the sun, moisturize regularly, and avoid harsh skincare products, intense heat, and strenuous exercise for a few days.

Common questions

What are the benefits?

+

Morpheus8 can improve skin texture, reduce wrinkles and fine lines, fade acne scars and stretch marks, and tighten the skin.


What are the potential side effects?

+

Common side effects include redness, swelling, pinpoint bleeding, and small scabs, which usually subside within a few days.

How many treatments will I need?

+

Typically, 1-3 treatments are recommended, spaced 4-6 weeks apart.

Is it painful?

+

Topical anesthesia is typically applied, and most patients report minimal discomfort, often described as a slight prickling or heat sensation.
