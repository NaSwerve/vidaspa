The treatment experience

Laser hair removal at VidaSpaNYC typically involves a consultation, the actual treatment, and post-care. During the consultation, the technician assesses your skin type and hair, and discusses the procedure. The treatment involves using a handheld device to deliver laser pulses, potentially causing a slight tingling sensation as it targets hair follicles.

Benefits:

  • Permanent hair reduction: Laser hair removal can significantly reduce hair growth over time. While it's not always perfectly permanent, many people experience a long-lasting reduction in hair, with some even achieving permanent hair removal.
  • Precision: Lasers can target specific hair follicles without damaging the surrounding skin, making it a precise method for hair removal.
  • Reduced Ingrown Hairs: By damaging or destroying hair follicles, laser hair removal can prevent future ingrown hairs, which are a common problem with other methods like shaving or waxing.
  • Cost-Effectiveness (Long-Term): While the initial cost of laser hair removal can be higher than other methods, the long-term savings from not needing to purchase razors, shaving cream, or waxing appointments can make it a more cost-effective option in the long run.
  • Improved Skin Texture: Laser hair removal can lead to smoother, clearer skin in the treated areas as it eliminates ingrown hairs and reduces the need for frequent shaving or waxing.

Pre and post care

What can I expect after my treatment?

+
After laser hair removal, expect some redness and slight swelling, similar to a mild sunburn, which should subside within a few hours to a day. You'll likely notice hair shedding within 1-3 weeks as targeted hairs exit the follicles.

What precautions should I take pre and post?

+

Before the treatment: Shave the area a day or two before the treatment to allow for some stubble but avoid long hairs, according to a video tutorial.


After the treatment: Avoid sun exposure. Keep the area hydrated. Avoid other hair removal methods for a while. Take cool showers.

Common questions

How does it work?

+

Laser hair removal uses concentrated light beams to target melanin (the pigment in hair).


The light energy is converted to heat, damaging the hair follicle and inhibiting future growth.


This process is most effective when hair is in its active growth phase (anagen), which is why multiple treatments are needed to target follicles at different growth stages.

Is it permanent?

+

Laser hair removal significantly reduces hair growth and can make hair finer and lighter, but it's not always a permanent solution.


Hormonal fluctuations can affect hair regrowth, and some individuals may experience some hair regrowth over time.


Results vary per person, and some may achieve 100% hair reduction while others may see some fine hairs grow back.

What are the risks?

+

Laser hair removal is generally considered safe when performed by a trained and qualified professional.


Certain medications, skin conditions, and medical history may make someone ineligible for treatment.

How many treatments will I need?

+

Most people need multiple laser hair removal sessions to achieve optimal results. Typically, 6-8 sessions spaced several weeks apart are recommended, according to multiple clinic resources.
