The treatment experience

A conductor gel is applied to the targeted area, ensuring smooth contact. The Ultraformer MPT handpiece is then placed on your skin, emitting precise micro-focused ultrasound energy where you might feel a gentle warming or tingling sensation. Our Aesthetician will start at the deepest skin layer, repeat the process again using different probes to target every skin layer and depth to ensure the best possible results. Your face will begin to feel tighter right after the appointment.

Pre and post care

What can I expect after my treatment?

+
After your treatment, you may experience some redness, swelling, or tenderness in the treated area, which typically resolves within a few hours to a few days. You may also notice an immediate tightening effect, with continued improvement over the following weeks as collagen production increases. Your skin will produce Collagen for up to 4 months.

What precautions should I take pre and post?

+
There are no special requirements for Ultraformer. We recommend taking vitamin C to help the metabolism synthesize collagen. You can apply makeup, exercise, and bathe as usual after your treatment. We always recommend using sunscreen to prevent sun damage to the skin. Sunscreen with an SPF of 30 or higher should be used daily. If you are exposed to the sun for prolonged periods, sunscreen needs to be reapplied at least every two hours.

Common questions

How does the Ultraformer work?

+

Ultraformer MPT is the newest upgraded generation of the Ultraformer device, using highly sophisticated Micro and Macro Ultrasound energy to rejuvenate three critical levels of your skin’s infrastructure. High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) transducers deliver concentrated waves of ultrasound into multiple layers of skin and body tissues without making direct contact with surrounding areas or damaging the surface. Transferring stabilized temperatures of 65-75°C into the targeted zones, the Ultraformer induces a process of neocollagenesis.


We use the Ultraformer MTP Technology, the world’s number one choice for non-invasive skin lifting & tightening treatments. It is the most advanced focused-ultrasound device with seven calibrated cartridge depths to elicit multi-layer contraction for facial contouring, wrinkle reduction, and overall skin laxity improvement.

What are the benefits?

+

Faster Treatment: With its Micro Pulsed Technology, the Ultraformer MPT provides faster treatment sessions compared to previous versions, reducing the time commitment while delivering effective results.


Enhanced Comfort: Clients often report a more comfortable experience with the Ultraformer MPT due to its improved handpieces and advanced technology, minimising any potential discomfort.


Personalised Treatment: The Ultraformer MPT's adaptability allows us to tailor treatments to each client's unique skin concerns and goals, ensuring a personalized experience.


No Needles or Surgery: Clients who seek effective results without the need for needles or surgery find the Ultraformer MPT to be an appealing option for achieving a youthful appearance.


What are the risks?

+

Ultraformer does not affect the outer layers of the skin, so there are minimal side effects or risks. However, due to the different levels of Ultraformer treatment available, side effects may vary. You may experience some mild swelling, tenderness or redness after your Ultraformer procedure, which will subside within 48 hours.


Ultraformer is not recommended during pregnancy or breastfeeding due to the ultrasound technology used.

How many treatments will I need?

+

Ultraformer has three differing levels, which target to three different depths of the skin. After an initial consultation the best suiting treatment level will be prescribed to you. Therefore, the recommended series of Ultraformer treatments vary from each client and each treatment level.

Is it painful?

+

You might feel a gentle warming sensation as the energy works to stimulate collagen production and tighten existing collagen fibers. The procedure is generally well-tolerated, with many individuals experiencing minimal discomfort. The Ultraformer MPT Technology provides a more comfortable, painless experience through its innovative Micro Pulsed Technology, tailored to suit various skin types. Those who have undergone different HIFU procedures using alternative devices have noted considerably reduced discomfort in treated regions on both the body and face when compared to previous experiences.
